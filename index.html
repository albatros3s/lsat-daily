How to study toward 170+

  • Always write your reasoning before revealing — at your level, points leak from process slips you can only fix if you can see them.
  • Necessary Assumption: use the negation test to break your final two. Negate logically, not dramatically.
  • Flaw: watch for absence of evidence ≠ evidence of absence, and answers that inflate a narrow claim into a universal one.
  • Accuracy before speed. Lock in the method now; add timing pressure once you are consistently right.
